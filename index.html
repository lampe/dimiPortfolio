    About

    Hi! I'm Dmitrij, a graphics designer with a fondness for computer games, specialized in 2D animation. During my studies at the Hamburg University of Applied Sciences I worked with traditional and digital methods to bring images to life! So far I applied my knowledge in multiple animated shorts and computer games as well as internships at advertising agencies in Hamburg.

    I'm offering my expertise with industry standard animation software like Adobe After Effects, TVPaint Animation and Spine. Also a bit of 3D modeling in Blender and sound design in Audacity. Drop me a message! I'd love to be part of your game projects or animated films!

    Void

    Dima Chotin Showreel from Dmitrij Chotin on Vimeo.

    This is a complete playthrough of my Bachelor project "Void". It's a multiple choice browser-game, wherein the story is conveyed completely through animations instead of text. You are controlling the thoughts of a crashed astronaut drifting alone in space. Depending on what thoughts the player selects, his mental condition cycles through manic and depressive spectra respectively. The original concept contains multiple branching paths and three alternate endings. This prototype can be downloaded HERE (about 360MB or so).

    • Date: July 2015
